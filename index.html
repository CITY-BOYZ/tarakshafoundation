About the Foundation

A research-backed institution building long-term, systemic solutions

Taraksha Foundation operates at the convergence of public health challenges and technological possibility. We pursue evidence-based approaches to cancer awareness, hygiene infrastructure, and digital health — with a commitment to scale, equity, and scientific rigor.

Core Values
Scientific Integrity Innovation Sustainability Accessibility Impact at Scale

Mission

To develop and deploy research-driven, scalable healthcare solutions that address critical public health gaps — with particular focus on cancer research, preventive infrastructure, and accessible diagnostics in underserved communities.

Vision

A world where geography, income, or systemic neglect no longer determines health outcomes — enabled by data-informed innovation, institutional collaboration, and self-sustaining technology ecosystems.

Key Initiatives

Where We Focus

Three interconnected areas of work, each addressing a distinct but reinforcing dimension of public health and sustainable impact.

01 / 03
Cancer Research & Awareness
Healthcare

Bridging the gap between research and community — with early-detection strategies, outreach in underserved regions, and a long-term ambition to contribute to affordable diagnostic pathways.

  • Early detection and screening frameworks for high-risk populations
  • Awareness campaigns in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities and rural districts
  • Collaboration pathways for accessible, cost-effective diagnostics
  • Patient education and navigational support resources
02 / 03
Public Health Initiatives
Public Health

Improving baseline hygiene infrastructure and reducing infection transmission risk in high-density environments — from government hospitals to public transit systems.

  • Hygiene infrastructure assessment in government health facilities
  • Infection prevention protocols for high-contact environments
  • Healthcare accessibility mapping and gap analysis
  • Partnerships with district health authorities for systemic change
03 / 03
Smart Hygiene Infrastructure
Innovation

Taraksha's flagship platform: an IoT-enabled, touchless dual-mode hand hygiene system combining handwash and sanitizer dispensing with an integrated digital display — engineered for continuous, self-sustaining operation.

  • Touchless dual-mode: handwash + sanitizer in a single unit
  • Embedded digital display generating advertisement-based monthly revenue per unit
  • Real-time IoT monitoring: usage analytics, low-supply alerts, uptime tracking
  • Self-sustaining model: ad revenue funds maintenance and expansion
  • Scalable deployment across hospitals, transit hubs, and public institutions

Why It Matters

The Urgency Behind
Our Work

Three converging crises demand structured, scalable responses — not piecemeal interventions.

1.4M
New cancer cases annually — India, WHO 2023

Rising Cancer Burden

India accounts for a disproportionate share of global cancer mortality, driven by late-stage diagnosis and limited access to affordable detection. Systemic early-intervention infrastructure remains critically underdeveloped in Tier 2 and rural regions.

62%
Government hospitals lack adequate hygiene infrastructure — NHSRC

Healthcare Infrastructure Gaps

A significant proportion of public health facilities operate without consistent, functional hand-hygiene access — directly contributing to healthcare-associated infections affecting both patients and clinical staff.

30%
HAIs preventable with hand hygiene — CDC Global

Preventable Through Hygiene

Globally, up to 30% of healthcare-associated infections are preventable through consistent hand hygiene practices. Touchless, monitored dispensing systems have demonstrated measurable compliance improvement in clinical studies.
